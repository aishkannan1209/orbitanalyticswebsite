What ORBIT Analytics delivers

Analytics built for
the way badminton is played

Six powerful analytics modules that go far beyond basic stats — built by players, for players.

🗺️
Court Coverage Heatmap
See exactly which zones each player dominates — and which they avoid. Identify coverage gaps in seconds.
🎯
Shot Selection Analysis
Understand shot preferences per rally, attack angles, and the patterns that win or lose points.
💥
Smash Zone Intelligence
Map where every smash originates and lands. Understand your strongest attacking positions vs opponents' weak zones.
🏸
Racket Contact Analysis
Pinpoint exact contact moments and timing patterns to understand stroke efficiency and consistency.
🔁
Match Pattern Recognition
Discover repeating tactical patterns across rallies — what sequences lead to winners or errors.
🎬
Annotated Video Export
Export highlight clips with visual overlays and annotations — ready to share with your coach or team.
